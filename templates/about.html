About Us

Based in Lagos, Nigeria, Creonovo is a family owned restaurant, focused on traditional recipes served with a modern twist.

The chefs draw inspiration from Calabar's soup culture and have a menu of 12-15 items that they rotate seasonally.

The restaurant has a rustic and relaxed atmosphere with moderate prices, making it a popular place for a meal any time of the day.

Creonovo is owned by Dafetite Ogaga, who is based in Lagos to pursue his dream of owning a restaurant.

To craft the menu, Dafetite relies on family recipes and his experience as a chef from around the world.

He sometimes join the marketing team to promote the restaurant and works to expand the menu beyond classic Nigerian dishes to incorporate additional cuisines from other countries.
